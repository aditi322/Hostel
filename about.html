Empowering Young Minds for a Better Tomorrow

Who We Are

Established in 1984, Vivekanand Education Society's Institute of Technology (VESIT) is one of the premier engineering colleges in Mumbai. Affiliated with the University of Mumbai and approved by AICTE, VESIT has been at the forefront of providing top-notch technical education and fostering innovation and research. With a sprawling campus in Chembur, Mumbai, VESIT offers an enriching academic environment for aspiring engineers, technologists, and entrepreneurs.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized institution for excellence in engineering and technological education, fostering innovation, and nurturing future leaders.

Our Mission

To provide quality education by integrating theoretical knowledge with practical applications, promoting research, and instilling ethical values to develop well-rounded professionals.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethical behavior in all our endeavors.

Innovation

Encouraging creativity and fostering a spirit of innovation among our students and faculty.

Excellence

Striving for excellence in education, research, and service to society.

Achievements

Over the years, VESIT has consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in Mumbai. Our students have excelled in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities, making a mark in national and international platforms. Our alumni are employed in prestigious organizations globally, contributing significantly to their respective industries.
